Hobbies

A few things I enjoy outside of school, work, and technology.

Painting

Painting has become one of my favorite creative outlets. I enjoy creating landscapes, nature scenes, and abstract artwork as a way to relax and express creativity outside of technology and fitness.

Hiking

Hiking allows me to stay active, explore new places, and enjoy the outdoors.

Snowboarding

Snowboarding is one of my favorite winter activities and gives me a chance to challenge myself while enjoying the outdoors.

Music

Music is another hobby I enjoy outside of technology and the outdoors. I like spending time playing guitar and keyboard whenever I get the chance.

Travel & Adventure

I enjoy traveling, getting outdoors, and trying new experiences. Some of my favorite memories have come from exploring Puerto Rico, including hiking through El Yunque National Forest, kayaking, ziplining over the rainforest, horseback riding on the beach, and walking through the streets of Old San Juan.
