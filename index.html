U.S. Navy Veteran • IT Student

Welcome to my personal website. I'm a Cincinnati native, U.S. Navy veteran, and Information Technology student at the University of Cincinnati with a focus on Cybersecurity.

After graduating from Taylor High School and Diamond Oaks Career Campus, I joined the Navy at 19 years old and completed three deployments in four years, including two during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Today I'm pursuing my Bachelor's degree, building my technical skills, working toward industry certifications, and preparing for a career in technology.
